I'm a big fan of animation but I don't really like to think of animation as a genre. It's a medium. And within that medium there is a huge array of possible styles and approaches. And honestly, a lot of animation can be a little boring and by the book.

What excites me so much about animation is that it's an artform with limitless possibilities for how to convey information, emotion or action. Animation presents filmmakers with an opportunity to display exactly what they want to on the screen. Nothing is incidental.
